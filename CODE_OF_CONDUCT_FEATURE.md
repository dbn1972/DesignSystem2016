## Features

### 1. **Our Pledge**
- Commitment to inclusive and harassment-free environment
- Open and welcoming community for all participants
- Diversity and inclusion values
- Equal treatment regardless of background

### 2. **Our Standards**
**Positive Behaviors:**
- ✅ Respectful and inclusive language
- ✅ Welcoming diverse perspectives
- ✅ Graceful acceptance of constructive feedback
- ✅ Focus on community benefit
- ✅ Empathy towards community members

**Unacceptable Behaviors:**
- ❌ Harassment and discriminatory language
- ❌ Personal or political attacks
- ❌ Public/private harassment
- ❌ Privacy violations
- ❌ Unprofessional conduct

### 3. **Expected Behavior**
- **Be Respectful:** Value all contributions and perspectives
- **Be Collaborative:** Work together towards common goals
- **Be Professional:** Maintain high standards of conduct
- **Be Accountable:** Take responsibility for actions
- **Be Inclusive:** Welcome and support all members

### 4. **Unacceptable Behavior**
**Zero Tolerance:**
- Harassment or discrimination
- Threats or violent language
- Deliberate intimidation
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Stalking or following

**Unprofessional Conduct:**
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Disrupting discussions
- Publishing private information
- Advocacy for unacceptable behavior

### 5. **Maintainer Responsibilities**
- Define and clarify acceptable behavior standards
- Take fair and appropriate corrective action
- Remove/reject inappropriate contributions
- Ban contributors for violations
- Enforce the Code of Conduct

### 6. **Scope**
Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (repositories, issues, PRs)
- Public spaces representing the project
- Official project communication channels
- Community events and gatherings
- Individual representation of the project

### 7. **Enforcement**
5-step enforcement process:
1. **Report Submission** - Via conduct@ux4g.gov.in
2. **Acknowledgment** - Within 24 hours
3. **Investigation** - Review evidence and context
4. **Decision** - Determine appropriate action
5. **Communication** - Notify all parties of outcome

**Response Time:** Within 24 hours for acknowledgment

### 8. **Reporting Violations**
**How to Report:**
- Email: conduct@ux4g.gov.in
- Include detailed description of incident
- Provide context and evidence
- Maintain confidentiality
- Expect fair review process

**What to Include:**
- Date, time, and location of incident
- Description of what happened
- Names of individuals involved
- Witnesses (if any)
- Supporting evidence (screenshots, links)

**Confidentiality:**
- All reports handled with discretion
- Reporters' privacy protected
- Information shared only as needed
- No retaliation against reporters

### 9. **Consequences**
Progressive discipline approach:

1. **First Violation:**
   - Written warning
   - Required apology
   - Temporary suspension (if severe)

2. **Second Violation:**
   - Temporary ban (7-30 days)
   - Public acknowledgment
   - Required behavioral changes

3. **Third Violation:**
   - Permanent ban from project
   - Removal of all contributions
   - Public announcement

**Severe Cases:**
- Immediate permanent ban
- Legal action if necessary
- Reporting to authorities

### 10. **Attribution**
Based on industry standards:
- Contributor Covenant v2.1
- Open Source Code of Conduct
- Government of India community guidelines
- Indian IT industry best practices
